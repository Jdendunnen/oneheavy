About OneHeavy
Unity Means Everything To Us

The World's Heaviest Music Hub

OneHeavy is a 100% original heavy music publication built for fans of heavy music worldwide. Every word on this site is written by us — band profiles, genre guides, festival deep dives, album reviews and Australian metal commentary in our own editorial voice.

Our Mission

Heavy music is one of the most passionate and dedicated music communities in the world. Yet finding genuinely thoughtful editorial coverage of the bands, scenes and stories that make this music matter has always been scattered across dozens of different sites, social media feeds and forums. Mainstream publications often treat heavy music as a niche curiosity. Specialist sites are sometimes too narrow in focus. Aggregators just rehash the same press releases.

OneHeavy was built to fix that. We are a genuine heavy music publication — original editorial content written by metal fans, for metal fans, with a particular focus on the extraordinary Australian heavy music scene. We cover the global metal world with the same passion and depth we apply to bands from our own backyard.

No paywalls. No subscriptions. No logins required. Just metal.

What We Publish

OneHeavy's editorial library covers everything from in-depth band profiles to historical features. Our band profiles are deep dives into the careers, music and significance of the bands we cover — with particular emphasis on Australian acts like Parkway Drive, Thy Art Is Murder, Ne Obliviscaris, Polaris, Caligula's Horse, The Amity Affliction, Karnivool, Voyager, Northlane and Hidden Intent.

Our genre guides explore the history and key bands of every major heavy music subgenre. Our festival coverage takes readers inside the world's greatest metal festivals from Wacken Open Air to Download. And our editorial features tackle the cultural questions that matter to heavy music fans — why Australian metal punches so far above its weight, what makes a great metal album, where the genre is heading next.

The Australian Scene

OneHeavy has a particular focus on the Australian heavy music community. Australia has produced some of the most artistically accomplished and commercially successful heavy bands of the past two decades — Parkway Drive, Thy Art Is Murder, Ne Obliviscaris, Polaris, The Amity Affliction, Karnivool, Voyager and many more. We believe this scene deserves a proper editorial home that takes it as seriously as any international publication.

Our dedicated city pages cover the metal scenes in Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Adelaide and Perth, with venue guides, promoter directories and a free show submission system. We also list every major Australian touring promoter — Hardline Media, Destroy All Lines, Live Nation Australia, Frontier Touring, Handsome Tours, Metropolis Touring, Secret Sounds and Soundworks Touring — covering everything from intimate club shows to major arena events.

Submit Your Show

OneHeavy offers 100% free show listings for bands and promoters. Whether you are a local unsigned band playing your first headline show or a major promoter announcing a national tour, we will list your event for free and put it in front of metal fans across Australia and beyond.

Show submissions are reviewed within 24 hours. To submit a show, use the Submit Your Show button on our homepage or email us directly at increasinglyinquisitive@gmail.com.

The Creator

OneHeavy is independently run by Josef Den Dunnen, a heavy music fan based in Adelaide, Australia. Every editorial piece on the site is written by Josef in his own voice. The site exists because the Australian and global heavy music scenes deserve a publication that takes them as seriously as the music itself does.

OneHeavy is not backed by any record label, venue, promoter or industry body. It is a genuine independent publication funded by reader support via Ko-fi, modest affiliate partnerships (notably Amazon Associates) and the goodwill of the metal community. Direct sponsorship and partnership enquiries are welcome.
