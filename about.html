Our Mission

Heavy metal is one of the most passionate and dedicated music communities in the world, yet finding reliable news, discovering upcoming shows and staying connected with the global metal scene has always been scattered across dozens of different websites.

OneHeavy was built to fix that. We pull live news from the world's leading metal publications every five minutes and provide a single free destination for every metal fan. No paywalls. No subscriptions. No logins. Just metal.

Our News Sources

We aggregate headlines from six of the world's most respected heavy metal publications, updated automatically every five minutes via RSS. Every story links back to the original publisher.

  • Blabbermouth (USA) -- The world's leading heavy metal news site
  • Loudwire (USA) -- Hard rock and heavy metal coverage
  • MetalSucks (USA) -- News, reviews and sharp commentary
  • Metal Hammer (UK) -- The world's greatest metal magazine since 1983
  • Revolver (USA) -- Heavy metal culture and features
  • Heavy Mag AU (Australia) -- Australia's home of heavy music

Australian Metal Scene

OneHeavy has a strong focus on the Australian metal scene. We list all major Australian touring promoters and provide a free platform for Australian bands and promoters to submit shows. Our Australian promoter directory includes Hardline Media, Destroy All Lines, Live Nation Australia, Frontier Touring, Handsome Tours, Metropolis Touring, Secret Sounds and Soundworks Touring.

Submit Your Show

OneHeavy offers 100% free show listings for bands and promoters. Submit your show via the button on our homepage or email us at increasinglyinquisitive@gmail.com. We review all submissions within 24 hours.

Weekly Digest

Every Friday night we send our Weekly Digest -- the seven biggest metal stories of the week plus concert announcements. Subscribe free from our homepage. We never spam and you can unsubscribe any time.
