About OneHeavy

The World's Heaviest Music Hub

All the metal news that matters, all the shows worth seeing, all in one place. Free forever for every metal fan on the planet.

Our Mission

Heavy metal is one of the most passionate and dedicated music communities in the world, yet finding reliable news, discovering upcoming shows and staying connected with the global metal scene has always been scattered across dozens of different websites, social media feeds and ticketing platforms.

OneHeavy was built to fix that. We pull live news from the world's leading metal publications every five minutes, aggregate concert listings from around the globe, and provide a single free destination for every metal fan — whether you're into old school thrash, modern deathcore, atmospheric black metal or anything in between.

No paywalls. No subscriptions. No logins required. Just metal.

Australian Metal Scene

OneHeavy has a strong focus on the Australian metal scene. We list all major Australian touring promoters and provide a dedicated platform for Australian bands, promoters and venues to submit shows for free listing. Australia has one of the most passionate metal communities in the world and we are proud to support it.

Our Australian promoter directory includes Hardline Media, Destroy All Lines, Live Nation Australia, Frontier Touring, Handsome Tours, Metropolis Touring, Secret Sounds and Soundworks Touring — covering everything from intimate club shows to major arena events.

Submit Your Show

OneHeavy offers 100% free show listings for bands and promoters. Whether you are a local unsigned band playing your first headline show or a major promoter announcing a national tour, we will list your event for free and put it in front of thousands of dedicated metal fans.

Weekly Digest

Every Friday night we send our Weekly Digest — a handpicked selection of the seven biggest metal stories of the week, plus concert announcements and tour news. Subscribe free from our homepage. We never spam and you can unsubscribe any time.
