About the Sydney Metal Scene

The Scene

Sydney's heavy music scene is one of the most diverse and active in the southern hemisphere. The city has been home to some of Australia's most internationally recognised metal acts, including Thy Art Is Murder, one of the most successful extreme metal bands to emerge from Australian shores. The scene spans everything from technical death metal to atmospheric black metal, metalcore, and everything in between.

Sydney's geographic spread means the metal scene is distributed across multiple pockets of the city, from the inner west venues of Marrickville and Newtown to the CBD and beyond. Each area has developed its own character and loyal following, contributing to a rich and varied heavy music culture.

History

Sydney has a long history of producing and hosting world-class heavy music. The city's pub rock culture of the 1970s and 80s gave way to a thriving underground metal scene in the 1990s that produced bands with genuine international profiles.

The legacy of Sydney's early metal scene can still be felt today in the city's passionate fanbase and its strong tradition of supporting both local talent and international touring acts. Sydney metal fans are among the most dedicated in the country.
