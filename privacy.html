Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

OneHeavy (oneheavy.net) is a heavy metal news aggregation and concert discovery platform. We aggregate publicly available news from leading metal publications and provide a central hub for the global metal community. OneHeavy is operated by Josef DenDunnen, Adelaide, South Australia, Australia.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at: [email protected]

2. Information We Collect

OneHeavy collects minimal personal information. The information we may collect includes:

  • Email addresses voluntarily submitted via our Weekly Digest signup form
  • Show submission details voluntarily provided by bands and promoters
  • Anonymous usage data collected via Google Analytics 4 (see section 4)
  • Standard server log data including IP addresses and browser types

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send our Weekly Digest newsletter to subscribers who have opted in
  • To review and potentially list show submissions from bands and promoters
  • To understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • To respond to enquiries sent via our contact form

We will never sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Google Analytics

OneHeavy uses Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous data about how visitors use our site. This includes pages visited, time on site, geographic region and device type. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. The data is anonymised and cannot be used to identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. Google AdSense

OneHeavy uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

6. Cookies

OneHeavy uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Google Analytics cookies to track anonymous site usage statistics
  • Google AdSense cookies for advertising personalisation

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our site.

7. Third Party Links

OneHeavy contains links to third party websites including news sources such as Blabbermouth, Loudwire, MetalSucks, Metal Hammer, Revolver and Heavy Mag AU, as well as concert promoter websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Data Retention

We retain email addresses of newsletter subscribers for as long as you remain subscribed. You may unsubscribe at any time by contacting us at [email protected]. Show submission data is retained for the purposes of maintaining our concert listings.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws including the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email protected].

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
