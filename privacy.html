Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

OneHeavy (oneheavy.net) is a heavy metal news aggregation and concert discovery platform operated by Josef Den Dunnen, Adelaide, South Australia, Australia. Contact us at: increasinglyinquisitive@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information including:

  • Email addresses submitted via our Weekly Digest signup
  • Show submission details provided by bands and promoters
  • Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics 4
  • Standard server log data including IP addresses and browser types

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to send our Weekly Digest newsletter, review show submissions, improve the site, and respond to enquiries. We will never sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Google Analytics

OneHeavy uses Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage data. This data cannot identify you personally. You can opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. Google AdSense

OneHeavy uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve personalised ads. You can opt out at Google Ads Settings.

6. Cookies

We use cookies for Google Analytics (anonymous usage stats) and Google AdSense (advertising). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Third Party Links

OneHeavy links to third party websites including Blabbermouth, Loudwire, MetalSucks, Metal Hammer, Revolver, Heavy Mag AU and concert promoter sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Data Retention

We retain newsletter subscriber emails until you unsubscribe. To unsubscribe contact increasinglyinquisitive@gmail.com. Show submission data is retained for concert listing purposes.

9. Your Rights

You may have rights to access, correct or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights contact increasinglyinquisitive@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact Us

For any questions about this privacy policy contact increasinglyinquisitive@gmail.com.
