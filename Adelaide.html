Local Metal Scene

Adelaide
Metal

Adelaide's metal scene punches well above its weight. Tight-knit, passionate and fiercely supportive of local talent, the Adelaide heavy music community is one of Australia's most dedicated.

The Scene

Adelaide has a proud history of producing heavy talent and a loyal fanbase that turns out in force for both local and international acts. The scene spans metal, hardcore and everything in between, centred around a cluster of iconic venues.
